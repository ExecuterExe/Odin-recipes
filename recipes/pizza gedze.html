Pizza gedze (geze)

Description

Pizza geese are the same kind of dumplings, but with toppings of
the pizza ingredients inside, which were featured in "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

Ingredients

  • Baking wheat flour - 390 grams
  • Water - 200 grams
  • Vegetable oil - 17 grams
  • Salt - 1 gram
  • Olives - 70 grams
  • Sausage - 100 grams
  • Hard cheese - 100 grams
  • 2 tomato - 200 grams
  • Pizza sauce - optional

Cooking steps

  1. Knead the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes
  2. Cut all the stuffing into small cubes
  3. Roll out the dough into a thin layer and cut out circles of 7 cm in diameter
    with a glass. Fill the prepared "circles" with the filling
  4. Place the gedze on a heated frying pan with 3 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  5. Fry the gedze on each side until crusty. Once crusted, pour half a cup of boiling water
    into the pan and braise the gedze under a lid until the water has evaporated.
