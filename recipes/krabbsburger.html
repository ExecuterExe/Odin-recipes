Krabbsburger

Description

The burger from the SpongeBob cartoon has become so iconic that only lazy people
have not tried to make it. Moreover, it is still on the children's menu in
some restaurants. Cooking a crab burger is not difficult at all.
You can do it for sure.

Ingredients

  • A small round bun made of wheat flour - 1
  • Pork and beef cutlet - 1
  • Cheese in slices - 1
  • Tomato - 1
  • Pickled cucumber - 1
  • Any kind of lettuce leaves and a few rings of sweet onions - 1
  • Ketchup and mustard - 1
  • Frying oil - 1

Cooking steps

  1. Add spices, salt and onions to the minced meat. Grate it on a grater so that the latter is less tangible
    Mix the mass with your hands and form it into a cutlet,
    the diameter of which coincides with the diameter of a bun
  2. Fry the meat "tortilla" on both sides for a few minutes each.
    It is important that the meat is completely cooked inside
  3. Cut the bun in half. Put a thin layer of ketchup mixed with mustard
    on the bottom of the bun. Also smear the sauce on top of the bun
  4. Place lettuce, small slices of cucumber and tomato, and rings of onion on the dressing.
  5. Cover everything with a slab of cheese and the other half of the bun
