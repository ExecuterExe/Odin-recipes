Ratatouille

Description

The burger from the SpongeBob cartoon has become so iconic that only lazy people
have not tried to make it. Moreover, it is still on the children's menu in
some restaurants. Cooking a crab burger is not difficult at all.
You can do it for sure.

Ingredients

Preparation of olive oil:

  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Olive oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Provencal herbs (ready to use dry mixture from the package) - 1 tablespoon

Sauce:

  • medium sized onion - 1
  • bell peppers - 3
  • bitter pepper - 1
  • Tomato (grind tomatoes through a meat grinder) - 400 grams
  • Provencal herbs - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Garlic - 1 clove
  • Salt, pepper

Vegetables:

  • Green zucchini - half
  • Yellow zucchini - half
  • eggplant - 1
  • tomato - 3

Cooking steps

  1. Prepare the olive oil. Pour 3 tablespoons of olive oil, chop 2 cloves of garlic and
    add 1 tablespoon of herbs. Leave it to stand while we prepare everything else
  2. Make the sauce. First, we send 1 hot pepper and 3 bell peppers to a preheated
    oven at 200 ° C for 10-15 minutes. Then get rid of the seeds and skin
  3. Chop the onion, fry until golden and add the chopped hot peppers and bell peppers
    without seeds or skin. Roast for a couple of minutes and add 400 grams of tomato,
    stew everything on medium heat for about 5 minutes
  4. Add 1/2 tablespoon of Provençal herbs, 1 clove of garlic. Salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Stir, stew for 1-2 minutes, and then put it all in a blender. We turn it into
    a homogeneous mass, put it into a mold that can be put in the oven.
  6. Approximately per pan you need: half green zucchini, half yellow zucchini,
    1 eggplant, 3 tomatoes
  7. Cut the vegetables into thin circles, about 2-3 mm thick. Place in the order you like
  8. Salt, pepper
  9. Our oil has already infused, pour the vegetables on top
  10. Cover with foil and put it in a preheated oven at 140° C for 2 hours
  11. Remove the foil, set it to 180° C and leave it in the oven for another 30 minutes
  12. Decorate the ready dish with herbs
