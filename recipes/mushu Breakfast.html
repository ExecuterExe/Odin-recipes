Mushu Breakfast

Description

Mushu Breakfast - is a delicious dish, but its presentation can make it really unique!
Try to prepare and decorate this dish in the style of the dragon Mushu,
the faithful companion of the main character of the cartoon "Mulan", and
surprise your guests not only with the taste, but also with the original design

Ingredients

  • Rice - 100 grams
  • Water - 200 milliliters
  • chicken eggs - 2
  • narrow plate of bacon - 1

Cooking steps

Primary preparation

  1. Boil rice in a pot with a thick bottom (ratio 1:2, where the first is groats, the second - water)
  2. Pan-fry the bacon until brown
  3. Make a classic frosting

Secondary cooking (like in the cartoon)

  • Place boiled rice on the bottom of the plate, add oil, salt and spices as desired
  • Carefully arrange the eggs at the top of the plate so that they resemble two eyes
  • Make a smile out of the fried bacon
